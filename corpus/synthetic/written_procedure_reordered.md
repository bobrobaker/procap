# LabRig start-up (reordered + gappy reference)

A deliberately flawed written procedure used to demonstrate the OFFLINE content audit. It
documents the valve **before** the pump (reordered), **omits** the flow-setpoint step
(gap), and adds a **calibrate** step the video never shows (extra). An offline lexical audit
of a procedure with filled intents should flag all three.

1. Open the main valve until valve open shows.
2. Start the feed pump and confirm pump running.
3. Calibrate the sensor array before first use.
