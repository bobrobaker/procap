# grill-me — feedback log

Dated provenance of how the grill-me protocol evolved: what was ambiguous or misapplied,
and the correction. Consult when **revising** a rule. The distilled, firing form lives in
`SKILL.md` (`## Gotchas` and the body) — this is the audit trail, not loaded on every run.

- **2026-06-11** — First version created from an alignment session where three spec drafts
  had missed a fundamental reframe of the goal. Interrogating through rounds, rather than
  accepting the initial framing, surfaced a full reframe in round one that spec drafts had
  not caught.
- **2026-06-11** — Overhauled after first live firing: merged the assumption-ledger
  approach, 3–5 independent prose questions per batch with recommended defaults, tentative-
  default handling, and artifact sections. Added the `--impl` depth argument. Replaced
  widget-style questions with prose batches. Anti-goal probing dialed to one light question
  asked once — an earlier version re-probed across rounds and made it a production, which
  was flagged as anti-goal fixation rather than grilling.
